Rules

  • The name must be in the song itself, not just the title.
  • The name cannot refer to someone famous (e.g., "Virginia Woolf" by the Indigo Girls).
  • For now, I'm only including English songs.
  • The song must refer to a person. For example, "Chelsea," by Counting Crows, would not fit because it refers to the city of Chelsea, NY, not to a girl named Chelsea.
  • If multiple artists have covered the same song, I will only include the original.
  • I don't vouch for the content or quality of the songs--only that they contain names.
